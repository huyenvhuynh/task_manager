ABOUT US

Our task management website provides a streamlined solution for students and professionals to organize their assignments and deadlines effectively. Users can add assignments by linking them to specific courses, making it easy to track tasks across different subjects or projects. The platform also includes a personalized calendar feature, allowing users to plan and visualize their workload. With a clear overview of upcoming assignments and deadlines, users can manage their time more efficiently and stay on top of their responsibilities.

FEATURES


Assignments

Add, track and organize tasks and deadlines easily.

Calendar

Clear overview of all your assignments, manage your time effectively.

Discussions

Collaborate with other users, share insights and ask questions.
